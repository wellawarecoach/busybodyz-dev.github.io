What Is a Reset?

A Reset is a short, focused sequence you perform on your own to reduce hidden tension, improve breathing mechanics, and restore how your body and nervous system work together.

It's not a workout.
It's not stretching.
And it's not about doing more.

It's about removing what's in the way.

When tension drops and your system recalibrates, strength feels smoother, recovery improves, and energy becomes more usable throughout the day.

You train to build capacity.
You Reset to protect and restore it.

The Four Reset Pillars

1) Nervous System

Downshift reactivity. Restore baseline calm.

2) Cognitive

Reduce mental load. Clear attention and decision fatigue.

3) Structural

Unload bracing. Improve breathing mechanics and position.

4) Sensory

Expand awareness. Reduce perceptual narrowing from stress.

How to Use Them

Pick the pillar your system needs most today. Do one Reset. Then stack it consistently. Small inputs compound into real capacity.
